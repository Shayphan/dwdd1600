Who Were the Signers?

Famous Signers

Some well known signers of The Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson, a spokesman for democracy, was an American Founding Father, the principal author of the Declaration of Independence, and the third President of the United States.
John Adams
John Adams, a remarkable political philosopher, served as the second President of the United States (1797-1801), after serving as the first Vice President under President George Washington.
Benjamin Franklin
Benjamin Franklin was an American polymath and Founding Father celebrated for his achievements as a scientist, diplomat, writer, and inventor. He is famously known for his groundbreaking electricity experiments and for helping draft the Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution.
John Hancock
John Hancock was the first to sign the the declaration of independence, and he was notoriously known for his extremely large signature on the document. He was an american statesman and a long time governer for the state of Massachusetts.
